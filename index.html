About Me

Jason, the owner of the park, grew up loving the outdoors – fishing, kayaking, mountain biking and camping. He spent much of his childhood wandering the woods of the vast Poconos in Pennsylvania. After a career as a decorated aviator and veteran of the Iraq and Afghanistan wars, Jason chose Texas as his home, and got himself an RV. He created Calhoun’s to provide families, outdoorsmen and campers with a friendly, laidback RV retreat.
